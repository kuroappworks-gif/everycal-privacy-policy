# Privacy Policy - EveryCal

**Last updated: December 5, 2025**

EveryCal (hereinafter referred to as "this App") respects the privacy of its users and is committed to protecting personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how user information is handled in this App.

## 1. Information Collected and Purpose of Use

### 1.1 Automatically Collected Information (Ads and Analytics)

This App uses the following third-party service providers for ad delivery and to improve app quality. These providers may automatically collect information such as user device information (e.g., Advertising ID), IP addresses, and crash logs for the purpose of displaying appropriate advertisements and analyzing app usage.

- **Google AdMob**  
  An advertising service provided by Google LLC. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.  
  - [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)  
  - [How Google uses data in ads](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)

### 1.2 Information Input by Users (Local Data)

Data entered when using the functions of this App (calculation history, memo function, checklists, etc.) is saved **only on the user's device** (local storage). This data is never sent to the developer's servers nor shared with third parties.

### 1.3 Use of External APIs

In the currency exchange rate calculation feature, requests are sent to external APIs (such as Frankfurter API) to retrieve the latest rates. **No personally identifiable information** is transmitted during this communication.

## 2. Sharing and Provision of Information to Third Parties

This App will not provide users' personal information to third parties, except in the following cases:

- With the user's consent.
- When required by law.
- When third-party services integrated into this App (AdMob) collect information for ad delivery or analysis.

## 3. Data Deletion

Data entered by the user (history, memos, etc.) is stored within the device. Uninstalling this App or executing **"Clear Data"** or **"Clear Storage"** from the app settings on your device will completely delete this data.

## 4. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

## 5. Changes to Privacy Policy

This policy may be revised without prior notice due to changes in laws or app functions. The revised policy shall become effective from the time it is posted on this page.
